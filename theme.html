Mission Statement

The Asian Christian Fellowship seeks to carry out the Great Commission of our Lord Jesus Christ to make disciples of all nations by proclaiming the Gospel, providing fellowship for edification, nurturing Christians in spiritual growth, and building up Christian leaders.

Our mission statement comes from Matthew 28:18-20, a passage known as the Great Commission. As Jesus' words after his resurrection, we believe that they should be as important to us as they were to Christ's apostles and disciples.

Theme for 2021-2022

The theme verse and theme are contextual ways in which we can understand our mission statement on both a personal and a fellowship level. This year, our theme is Anchored.

“until we all attain to the unity of the faith and of the knowledge of the Son of God, to mature manhood,
     to the measure of the stature of the fullness of Christ, so that we may no longer be children, tossed to and fro by the waves and carried about by every wind of doctrine, by human cunning, by craftiness in deceitful schemes.
- Ephesians 6:13-14 (ESV)

Our theme verse, Ephesians 4:13-16 points to our fellowship’s objective in seeking Christ, both individually and corporately. Our aim is for all ACFers to truly realize that they are God's beloved, and to be equipped with His Word, the truth that transcends the distractions and entangling controversies of this world. This frees us to seek God and love one another for our eternal joy and His eternal glory.
