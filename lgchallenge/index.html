ACF Large Group S14

Weekly Challenges

Starting from Large Group 3, there will be challenges issued for you to complete. The challenges will be strongly tied to the theme of that large group.

The purpose of these challenges is to encourage and motivate you to live out what you learn. We must not let ourselves be too preoccupied with simply "learning", but we must also be "doers of God's word".

At the end of the semester, for each person who has at least one submission, Jon will compile and collate the submissions for that individual without reading any of the contents. He will print it, put it into an envelope and seal it, and pass it to you.

This is between you and God. Your progress is not for Jon nor for MT to know.

Warning!

Do not attempt these with a heart of legalism. Instead, realize how hard it is for us to actually do these things, acknowledge how much we need God, and strive to keep doing them beyond!

Recovering God's Will Mar 21

  1. Read James 4:13-17. Let the Scriptures speak to you and repent of anything that convicts you.
  2. 1 Thess 5:18: Give thanks in everything, for this is God’s will for you in Christ Jesus.

Recovering Worship Feb 28

  1. Read Isaiah 66. Behold the glory of God that is painted across the verses. Repent of your detestable practices (v3) and idolatry. Rejoice (v14) and worship God (v23).
  2. Each time you encounter something good, glorify God. Each time you suffer, glorify God.
  3. By yourself, or with some friends, put on some music and worship God with your hearts, minds, and/or voices!

Recovering Fellowship Feb 21

  1. Read Acts 2. In prayer, repent if you have quenched the Spirit (1 Th 5:19), and ask God to fill ACF with the Holy Spirit.
  2. At an informal meeting (e.g. homework party at pool side, chilling at someone’s apartment) with other ACFers (or Christians) this week, start with corporate prayer to ask for wisdom for fellowship.
  3. Ask someone not from your class what they are struggling with this week. What should you do next?

Recovering Love Feb 14

  1. Read and re-read 1 John. Repent of our lack of love for God, and love Him with all our heart, mind, soul, and strength.
  2. Pray for ACF, that we can be more loving as a fellowship.
  3. Find someone who feels unloved, and love them in a tangible way.
