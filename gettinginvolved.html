As members of a body of believers, we are all called to serve God and serve one another. No matter what gifts you have been given or where you are in your walk, we hope that you are able to serve with what you have, be it in a church or in a fellowship. There are many ways in which you can serve the body, regardless of whether or not you are in a formal position. However, there are indeed many opportunities to serve in ACF. You can view them here below, with each ministry or team being explained by its leaders or coordinators. As an overall note, we would really appreciate it if you have any feedback for any particular ministry! Whether its questions, thoughts, suggestions, or comments, feel free to let these people know. If there are any questions or comments about ACF as a whole, you can direct your questions to Bryan Tan.

Welcome Team

Contact: Jon Yu and Wing Tung Wong

Welcome Team seeks to welcome newcomers and oldcomers alike to the body of Christ, as well as the ACF family. We want to embrace all who are interested and foster and nurture the body of Christ so that they may feel welcomed and feel comfortable enough to open their hearts and focus their minds solely on Christ. Ultimately, we want to encourage a welcoming culture in ACF, so that the both newcomers and oldcomers are not just welcomed by the team, but by the family as a whole.
We typically serve the welcome table on Friday nights to welcome people and give them name tags. However, we hope to broaden the role of Welcome Team this coming year. Some ideas for this would be to have members of Welcome Team stay inside the large group room to welcome people, having pre-large group eatups, and focusing on following up with newcomers. If you are looking to serve in this capacity, or if you have any suggestions or thoughts on how we can do better, please contact us! For more information about Welcome Team, take a look at this document.


Spur Team

Contact: Hannah Ng and Sarah Kwan

We are called by God to spur our brothers and sisters in Christ towards love and good deeds. Spur team seeks to cultivate that culture and spirit of encouragement throughout the members of ACF. As a body of Christ, we will be able to see the importance of spurring one another on during the seasons of everyone's different lives.
This year, Spur Team is planning and preparing various projects which include: Spur Note writing (year-round/Winter Retreat), birthday cards, a spur box, and other projects. Spur Team also hopes to incorporate more ACFers from Pitt, ACF sports meetups, and other activities that will help us spur one another physically, mentally/emotionally, and spiritually upward. If you have any ideas, or would like to help out in any capacity, please contact us!

Empower

Contact: Jon Yee

This ministry is concerned with empowering ACF through the Bible. We do this because we have received a gospel of righteousness through Jesus Christ. Jesus Christ suffered, died, and rose again, and His righteousness is now imputed to us. We are new creations, and it is not us who live but Christ who lives in us. Therefore, we must realize that it is Jesus Christ who empowers us, not man, but it is man who studies and grows and applies the teachings of Jesus, which includes all Scripture. Empower hopes to encourage this through weekly Bible studies and discussion groups. You can serve in this ministry by helping lead or facilitate discussion groups.

Exalt

Contact: Dan Mock

Therefore, I urge you, brothers and sisters, in view of God's mercy, to offer your bodies as a living sacrifice, holy and pleasing to God--this is your true and proper worship.
- Romans 12:1

Exalt is the worship ministry of Asian Christian Fellowship. What, then, is worship? One brief definition would be such: Worship is "giving glory to that which deserves glory". For us at ACF, the subject that we believe deserves this glory is God. So put simply, worship is giving glory to God.
There are four main ways the Exalt ministry serves ACF:

  • Worship Team - responsible for leading times of musical worship during Large Group and other ACF gatherings.
  • Audio/Visual Team - responsible for the setup/breakdown and transportation of sound equipment, and other technical details, at ACF gatherings.
  • Slides Person - responsible for running slides during ACF events and in most cases, creating the slides as well.
  • PCC Worship Team - responsible for leading times of musical worship the PCC English Service, about once a month.

If you are interested in getting involved with Exalt, have ideas for other ways that ACF can express worship to our God, or are simply just interested in learning more about worship and what it is, send an email to me!


Cell Groups

Contact: and Amanda Ko

The cell ministry is about fostering a place for Christian brethren to come together in a comfortable setting to share, pray, be vulnerable and accountable, study the Word together, and grow together -- in essence, a place to be a family together chasing after the one goal of pointing upwards toward Christ. If you feel a burden to care for your Christian brethren in an intentional and focused manner, consider becoming a cell leader! (If explicit leadership isn't your thing, you can also sign up to assist cell leaders in implementing this vision of family.)
